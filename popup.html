Our Mission and Research

Our Mission

Eco Streaming empowers users to understand the environmental impact of their online streaming activity.
By reducing your own carbon footprint through streaming, you can better understand the impact that video streaming services have on the environment and reduce your carbon output. We also encourage you to sign our petitions, holding companies like Amazon and Netflix responsible for reducing their own environmental impact.

Research

Dr Chris Priest's work adopts a bottom-up approach in evaluating the Life-Cycle Assesment (LCA) of various online services. His work in the University of Bristol has had a profound impact in better monitoring and improving the sustainable design of these digital serivices. Below is a link to his work with modeling the CO2 emissions of the music industry on YouTube and proposals on how to improve it as well as a link to his paper on Evaluating Sustainable Interaction Design of Digital Services: The Case of YouTube (Chris Priest/ Dan Schien).
